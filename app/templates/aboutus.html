Purpose of the project

  • Specific topic: Examining climate change and its impacts around the world; at different scales, local, national and global. Specifically different events such as, wildfires, tornados, heat waves, droughts, floods, hurricanes, and melting polar ice caps.
  • Why does it matter to Me?: I am curious about the impacts climate change on the environment and want to learn about ways to address the damages done.
  • Why does it matter to other people?: It matters to other people because weather and climate patterns can put lives at risk. There have been far more extreme floods, tornadoes, wildfires and many more natural disasters. People want to know how their homes will be affected.
